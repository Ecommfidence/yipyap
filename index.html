For Kids

Pipsqueak is a Bluetooth extension phone designed specifically for kids (ages 3+.) Connecting to your phone like a wireless Bluetooth headset, Pipsqueak will work with nearly every cell phone out there, including iPhones and Androids, allowing mom or dad to route an incoming call directly to their child.

Save Money

No more handing over expensive smartphones to kids for them to talk to Gramma, now they can have their own phone to talk on, play with and make their very own.
